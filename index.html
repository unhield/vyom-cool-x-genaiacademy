Team Description

Data Alchemists convert satellite, climate, and civic data into AI decision tools for safer cities. VYOM-COOL AI detects heat hotspots, simulates cooling strategies, and ranks interventions for climate-resilient communities.

Brief Description

VYOM-COOL AI is a GenAI-powered urban heat decision intelligence platform for city planners and communities. It combines satellite-derived land surface temperature, vegetation, population exposure, and built-up indicators to identify heat hotspots, explain risk drivers, simulate cooling interventions, and recommend priority actions for Ahmedabad and Rajkot.

Demo Storyline

  1. Open the Intelligence Map and select Ahmedabad Ward A.
  2. Show CACPI score, heat drivers, exposed population, and LST risk.
  3. Run the Scenario Simulator with combined cooling strategies.
  4. Open the Portfolio Optimizer and explain the recommended sequence.
  5. Ask Gemini for a ward action brief and show the generated recommendation.
